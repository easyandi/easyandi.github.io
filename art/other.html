Works dealing with the reproduction of pornography.
 
The artworks shown here represent an effort to engage with the aesthetics of pornography in reproduction, firstly as a printed artefact, and then in a digital and screen form.
 
The 'explicit nature' of some of the material below once cost me an entire website, together with an associated email account and 10 years of electronic correspondence. My argument to the website host - that I was an artist and not a pornographer - must have sounded like a one thousand year-old cliche and the truth of it fell on deaf ears. For this reason, I've put these artworks discreetly at the bottom of this page and, while I don't actually hold to the idea of a comfortable separation between art and pornography, I do hereby consider sensitive souls to have been forewarned.
